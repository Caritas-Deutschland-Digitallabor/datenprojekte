# deliberAIde - The AI Co-Pilot for Democratic Participation

## Kurzbeschreibung
An innovative AI assistant for more inclusive, effective and sustainable communal participation. Adaptable across various formats and use cases, the Co-Pilot can aid with diverse tasks like live translation, summarization, and considering long-term and environmental impacts.
